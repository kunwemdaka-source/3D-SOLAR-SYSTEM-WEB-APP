Mission Achievements

First Planetary Flyby

Mariner 2 successfully flew by Venus in 1962, becoming the first spacecraft to visit another planet.

Moon Landing

Apollo 11 landed humans on the Moon in 1969, achieving humanity's first steps on another celestial body.

Mars Exploration

Multiple rovers have explored Mars, with Perseverance currently searching for signs of ancient life.

Outer Planet Exploration

Voyager and Cassini missions provided unprecedented views and data about the gas giants.

Interstellar Space

Voyager 1 became the first human-made object to enter interstellar space in 2012.

Sample Return

OSIRIS-REx successfully collected samples from asteroid Bennu, returning them to Earth in 2023.
